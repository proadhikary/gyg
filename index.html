Frequently Asked Questions

Who are the mentors on Get Your Guidance?

All our mentors are current students at top international universities. They are pursuing the very courses and programs you're aiming for and have firsthand experience with the admissions process, campus life, and academics.

How do I connect with a mentor?

You can fill up your choices in a google form which will be provided on the website. Once you submit your response, we will assign a mentor best suited to your needs.

Can I talk to more than one mentor?

Absolutely! In fact, we encourage it. Different mentors offer different perspectives, and connecting with multiple mentors can give you a more well-rounded understanding.

Is there a cost for mentorship sessions?

Yes, mentorship sessions are paid services.

How do I become a mentor?

If you're currently studying abroad and want to help others while earning, you can apply to be a mentor through our "Become a Mentor" section. We review applications carefully to ensure quality guidance for our users.

About Us

Get Your Guidance is a student-led platform that bridges the gap between study-abroad aspirants and students currently pursuing their dream courses at top global universities.
