About Us

Why Us?

Student-Led Mentorship

Get Your Guidance is a student-led platform that bridges the gap between study-abroad aspirants and students currently pursuing their dream courses at top global universities. We believe that the best advice comes from experience—and who better to guide you than someone who's already on the journey you want to take?

Whether you're choosing between universities, preparing your application, or adjusting to life abroad, your mentor is here to support you every step of the way.

Our Vision

Democratizing Access to Quality Guidance

We envision a world where every student has access to personalized, high-quality guidance for their educational journey, regardless of their background or resources. Traditional college counseling is often out of touch and expensive, creating barriers for many talented students.

Our mission is to make expert guidance accessible through near-peer mentorship, connecting students with mentors who have successfully navigated the same path. We believe this approach provides more relevant, up-to-date, and relatable guidance than traditional counseling services.
